Our purpose

The goal of this online encyclopedia is to collect, organize, and present reliable information about the world of video games. It aims to serve as a knowledge base for players, researchers, and anyone interested in the history, culture, and future of gaming.

To achieve our goal we:

  • Provide clear and structured articles about different game genres, platforms, and developers.
  • Highlight the most influential games and iconic characters that shaped the industry.
  • Offer insights into the cultural, social, and technological impact of video games.
  • Continuously expand and update content to reflect the latest trends and innovations in gaming.

About subject area

Video games represent one of the most dynamic and rapidly evolving areas of modern digital culture. From simple arcade machines to complex virtual worlds and esports competitions, games have become a global phenomenon that unites millions of people. The encyclopedia covers both the historical development of video games and their role in contemporary society.

Authors

This encyclopedia is created by a group of students and enthusiasts who are passionate about video games and their influence on technology, culture, and entertainment. Their shared mission is to make knowledge about gaming accessible to everyone.

Fun Facts about Video Games

Did you know?
  • The first commercially successful video game was Pong, released in 1972.
  • Super Mario Bros. is one of the best-selling games of all time, with more than 40 million copies sold.
  • The longest gaming marathon on a single game lasted over 35 hours.
  • The world’s most expensive video game ever developed is Grand Theft Auto V, with a budget of over $250 million.
  • Tetris has been played by hundreds of millions of people across almost every gaming platform.
